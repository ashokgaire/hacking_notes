====== encoding ======
Created Tuesday 14 July 2020


**#** Separate anchors **%23**
**?** Separate query string **%3F**
**&** Separate query elements **%24**
**%** Indicates an encoded character **%25**
**/** Separate domain and directories **%2F**
**+** Indicates a space **%2B**
Not recommended **%20 or +**
=       %3D
